About me

"The only place where success comes before work is in the dictionary." – Vidal Sassoon

I am Justin Daschner and curently a Full Stack Software Developer. I have been programming since beginning of highschool. Initially starting with Java as my first language, I began modding games such as Minecraft. Once I graduated highschool, I wanted to further pursue Computer Science.

In college, I given more oppertunities from helping start the Cyber Security Club at Virginia Commonwealth University to exploring my own interests in automation. At the end of college, we were given a chance to participate in real world problems with local companies for our senior projects. I worked in a team and CoStar to utilize Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning to create a program which could take in a morgage website page and identify key data points to populate a database. Our team won first place at the senior project expo.

After college, my goal was to work with a company as full time to gain more experience as a professional. On my birthday in 2019, I started with Patient First as a Junior Full Stack Software Developer. I never had worked with C# and .Net before and as my work place required it and understood I had a Java background, hired me and gave me chance to learn the framework. I was grateful for this oppertunity and within two weeks I became fluent in the .Net and .Net Core frameworks as a Full Stack Developer and been working at Patient First ever since.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, going to the gym, and spending time with my two border collies named Zoe and Gwen. I also spend time learning new things that I often don't get to encounter in my work such as Node.Js, Unity, and Machine Learning.
